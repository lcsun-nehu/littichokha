Our Manifesto

Here is the official promise-document for the 2026-2027 next academic term, guaranteed to bring development, dignity and thekuas.

1. Gastronomy & Mess Affairs

  • The Litti Chokha Act: As promised, Litti Chokha (with extra desi ghee) will be made a mandatory item in all hostel mess menus, to be served twice a week.
  • Sattu Security: A 24/7 Sattu Dispensing Machine (SDM) will be installed outside the Central Library for late-night study sessions.
  • The Bhujia Mandate: A formal inquiry will be launched into the campus canteens that serve Poha or Chowmein without a sufficient layer of bhujia.

2. Cultural & Festival Affairs

  • The Thekua Protocol (Code: Chhath): Any student returning from Bihar after Chhath Puja must declare and distribute their Thekua and Khajoor rations.
  • Punishment for Defaulters: Those who fail to bring Thekua will face a 'cultural fine': They must listen to a 3-hour loop of "Sorry Sorry" and write a 500-word essay.

3. Special Provisions

  • The 10% B.G. Quota: A 10% reservation in the main NEHUSU will be lobbied for Bihari Gays, ensuring our discourse is diverse and pro-homosexuality.
  • NRB Status (Non-Resident Bihari): Students of Bihari origin will be granted special NRB status.
  • NRB Perk 1: The right to play Bhojpuri songs in events organised by any department of NEHU.
  • NRB Perk 2: The right to go back to Bihar during Chhath even when there is no holiday provided by NEHU.

4. Infrastructure & Integration

  • The Chabutra Initiative: We will lobby for the construction of three (3) strategically placed chabutras (circular sitting platforms) near the canteens for all students.
  • Aesthetic Ghamcha Racks: To promote campus beautification, we will request "official," aesthetically-pleasing drying racks, improving the view for all.
  • High-Speed 'Proof-of-Life' Wi-Fi: We demand a high-bandwidth Wi-Fi zone, not for gaming, but for making buffer-free video calls home to families in any state.
